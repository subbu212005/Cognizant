Community Portal Help Center

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal Help Page. Here you can find basic instructions on how to use our digital services.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register for an event? Fill out the Event Registration Form on the home page, select your desired event, and click Register.
  • Can I save my preferences? Yes, selecting an event type saves your preference automatically. You can clear it using the "Clear Preferences" button.
  • How does Geolocation work? The "Find Nearby Events" button uses your browser's secure location service to locate the nearest community activities.
